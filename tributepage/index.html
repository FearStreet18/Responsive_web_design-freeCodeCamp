Mac Miller

The musician that never got the spotlight

Here is a timeline of Mr. Mac Miller's life:

  • 1992- Mac Miller was born in Points Breeze,Pittsburgh.
  • 2010- Miller signed with the independent label Rostrum Records.
  • 2011- Miller released his fifth mixtape, Best Day Ever.
  • 2012- Miller released his seventh mixtape, Macadelic.The single "Loud" peaked at number 53 on the Billboard Hot 100.
  • 2013- Miller founded the record label imprint REMember Music, named after a deceased friend.
  • 2014- Miller parted ways with Rostrum Records when his contract expired in January 2014.On May 11, 2014, Miller independently released his tenth solo mixtape, Faces.
  • 2016- His fourth studio album, The Divine Feminine, was released on September 16, 2016.The album features Miller singing nearly as much as rapping, and incorporates genres such as R&B, jazz and funk.
  • 2018- Miller's fifth studio album, Swimming, was released on August 3, 2018, to positive reviews from critics.Pitchfork described the album as consisting of "wistful soul and warm funk", through his exploration of heartbreak and his own mental health issues.Swimming debuted at number three on the Billboard 200 with 66,000 units, his fifth consecutive top five-charting album release in the United States.After his death in September 2018, the single "Self Care" rose to number 33 on the Billboard Hot 100, his highest peak as a lead artist at the time.Swimming was nominated for Best Rap Album at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards.
  • 2018- On September 7, 2018, Miller was found unresponsive in his Studio City home by his personal assistant.
